Issue 3009: Erroneous display of CSS inheritance

Text

Shorthand properties overwriting other shorthand property

  1. Inspect the yellow DIV (#div1)
  2. Switch to the Style Side Panel
  • Observed: The styles of #div1 are not marked as overwritten
  • Expected: The styles of #output div are overwriting the border-top styles of #div1, so they should be striked out

Shorthand property overwriting single-value properties

  1. Inspect the green DIV (#div2)
  2. Switch to the Style Side Panel
  • Observed: The styles of #output div are not marked as overwritten
  • Expected: The border style of #output div2 is overwriting all former styles related to the borders, so they should be striked out

Single-value property overwriting part of shorthand property

  1. Inspect the red DIV (#div3)
  2. Switch to the Style Side Panel
  • Observed: The styles of #div1 div are not marked as overwritten
  • Expected: The border-top-color style of #output div3 is overwriting the border color of the shorthand property border-top of #div1 div, so it should be striked out
